What is Comex Trading?

The Comex market, a division of the New York Mercantile Exchange (NYMEX), deals with the trading of metals futures and options contracts. It specializes in commodities like gold, silver, copper, and platinum. Comex provides a global platform for investors, speculators, and hedgers to trade based on their price movements. It's vital for setting global benchmark prices for precious metals.

What is Forex Trading?

The Forex market, the largest and most liquid financial market globally, facilitates the exchange of currencies. Operating 24 hours a day across time zones, it boasts an average daily trading volume of $6 trillion. The market is influenced by economic indicators, geopolitical events, and market sentiment, offering countless opportunities for traders.
